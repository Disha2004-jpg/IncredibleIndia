Must-Visit Places in Pune

Pataleshwar Cave Temple

A rock-cut cave temple from the 8th century, dedicated to Lord Shiva.

Raja Dinkar Kelkar Museum

A fascinating collection of artifacts from the 14th century.

Parvati Hill and Temple

A scenic hilltop temple offering breathtaking views of Pune.

Sarasbaug-Peshve Park

A beautiful garden with a historic temple and boating facilities.

Khadakwasla Dam

A scenic dam perfect for relaxing picnics and beautiful sunsets.

Shivneri Fort

The birthplace of Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj, a historical marvel.
