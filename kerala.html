Must-Visit Places in Kerala

Alleppey (Alappuzha)

Known as the "Venice of the East," famous for houseboat cruises and serene backwaters.

Kovalam Beach

A scenic beach near Thiruvananthapuram, famous for coconut groves and golden sand.

Thekkady

Home to Periyar National Park, perfect for wildlife spotting and boat safaris.

Wayanad

A lush green district with wildlife sanctuaries, spice plantations, and the Banasura Sagar Dam.

Kochi (Cochin)

A port city blending Indian and European cultures, known for its Chinese fishing nets.

Munnar

A breathtaking hill station with tea plantations, misty valleys, and cool weather.

Vagamon

A lesser-known hill station with lush forests, waterfalls, and scenic views.

Thiruvananthapuram

The capital of Kerala, home to the famous Sri Padmanabhaswamy Temple.

Exciting Things to Do in Kerala

Houseboat Cruises

Explore Kerala's peaceful backwaters on a luxurious houseboat.

Wildlife Safaris

Spot tigers, elephants, and deer in Periyar National Park.

Kathakali Performances

Watch Kerala’s famous dance drama with colorful costumes and expressions.

Ayurvedic Treatments

Experience Kerala's world-famous Ayurveda therapies for wellness.

Spice Plantation Tours

Visit spice farms and learn about Kerala’s famous spices like cardamom & pepper.

Beach Relaxation

Enjoy sunbathing at Kovalam, Varkala, or Cherai Beach.
