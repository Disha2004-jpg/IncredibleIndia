Must-Visit Places in Delhi

Red Fort

A UNESCO World Heritage Site and a symbol of India's history and freedom struggle.

Qutub Minar

A 73-meter tall tower built in the 12th century, a marvel of Indo-Islamic architecture.

Akshardham Temple

A stunning Hindu temple showcasing Indian culture, spirituality, and traditions.

Rashtrapati Bhavan

The official residence of the President of India with stunning Mughal Gardens.

Lodhi Garden

A peaceful garden with historical tombs and a beautiful landscape.

National Zoological Park

A home to various species of animals and birds, a fun spot for families.

National Museum

One of India’s largest museums, showcasing artifacts from across history.

National Crafts Museum

Features Indian handicrafts, textiles, and traditional arts.

Shopping in Delhi

Explore vibrant markets like Khan Market, Janpath, and Chandni Chowk.
