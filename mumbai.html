Must-Visit Places in Mumbai

Gateway of India

A famous arch monument built in 1924 to commemorate King George V and Queen Mary’s visit.

Chhatrapati Shivaji Terminus

A well-known railway station and a UNESCO World Heritage Site.

Bollywood

The birthplace of Bollywood, home to many famous actors and studios.

Shopping in Mumbai

From upscale shopping malls to street shopping at Fashion Street and Linking Road.

Mumbai Nightlife

Experience the vibrant nightlife at clubs, lounges, and beachside cafes.

Bandra-Worli Sea Link

A stunning cable-stayed bridge connecting Bandra and Worli.

Siddhivinayak Temple

A popular Hindu temple dedicated to Lord Ganesha.

Haji Ali Dargah

A beautiful mosque and dargah located in the middle of the sea.

Chhatrapati Shivaji Museum

A museum showcasing India's ancient history and artifacts.
